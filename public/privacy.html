Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-13

KidFinance is a family tool to help children build discipline and financial literacy. The app stores all user data locally on your device (LocalStorage/IndexedDB). We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

Data we store locally

  • Child profile (name you enter, age, settings)
  • Tasks, routines, workout builder and daily results
  • Rewards settings and history saved on this device

No accounts, no tracking

  • No user accounts or server sync
  • No analytics or third-party trackers
  • No push to our servers

Control & deletion

You can delete all data by clearing the site/app data in your browser settings or by uninstalling the installed app (PWA). If we add cloud sync in the future, this policy will be updated and explicit consent will be required.

Children

KidFinance is intended to be used by parents/guardians together with children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at /privacy.html.
